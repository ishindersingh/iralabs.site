Founder Role & Equity Proposal

Joint Venture Partnership Agreement
IRA Labs & TechHCad

1. Executive Overview

This proposal establishes the operational framework, resource allocation, compensation structure, and equity distribution for the joint venture between IRA Labs and TechHCad. The document outlines my role as operational founder, the investment and support provided by TechHCad, and the rationale for the proposed equity split that ensures sustainable growth and mutual success.

2. Strategic Context & Academic Considerations

To commit full-time operational leadership to this venture while maintaining academic obligations, I require formal employment documentation from TechHCad. This enables me to:

  • Obtain University NOC: Secure a No Objection Certificate from my university that permits full-time professional work alongside degree completion
  • Academic compliance: Demonstrate legitimate employment status required by institutional regulations
  • Professional credibility: Establish formal employment relationship that validates my commitment to both academic and professional responsibilities
  • Legal clarity: Create proper documentation for all stakeholders including family, university administration, and business partners

This arrangement allows me to dedicate complete operational focus to the venture while maintaining academic progress, ensuring neither commitment compromises the other.

3. Founder Responsibilities & Operational Scope

As the operational founder, I will assume comprehensive responsibility for building, managing, and scaling all core business functions:

3.1 Product Development & Content Creation

  • Designing comprehensive curriculum for AI, robotics, electronics, and coding programs
  • Recording, editing, and producing professional video course content
  • Developing both online and offline educational materials
  • Creating supporting documentation, tutorials, and learning resources

3.2 Operations & Team Management

  • Managing daily operational workflow and project execution
  • Recruiting and overseeing interns, trainers, video editors, and support staff
  • Establishing operational systems, processes, and quality standards
  • Coordinating cross-functional activities and ensuring deadline adherence

3.3 Marketing & Brand Development

  • Developing and executing comprehensive marketing strategies
  • Managing social media growth across YouTube, Instagram, LinkedIn, and other platforms
  • Creating content calendars, campaigns, and engagement systems
  • Building brand authority and community presence in the AI & robotics education sector
  • Website management, SEO optimization, and digital marketing automation

3.4 Research, Development & Product Innovation

  • Conducting R&D for educational robotics kits and AI projects
  • Designing electronics products and learning tools
  • Managing vendor relationships, production processes, and inventory control
  • Ensuring product quality standards and continuous improvement

3.5 Community Engagement & Business Development

  • Organizing workshops, demonstrations, and educational events
  • Developing school partnership programs and institutional relationships
  • Managing customer support, pre-order systems, and retention strategies
  • Creating pitch decks, business presentations, and strategic documentation

4. TechHCad Investment & Resource Commitment

TechHCad will provide the financial infrastructure and resources necessary for successful launch and sustainable growth:

4.1 Financial Investment

  • Complete funding for initial setup, equipment, and operational expenses
  • Monthly salary budget for founder and staff
  • Marketing and advertising budget allocation
  • Working capital for inventory, materials, and production costs

4.2 Infrastructure & Facilities

  • Office and laboratory space for operations, production, and content creation
  • Recording studio setup with professional video and audio equipment
  • Hardware, tools, and robotics materials for product development
  • Technology infrastructure including computers, software, and networking

4.3 Corporate Support & Network Access

  • Legal support for company registration, compliance, and contracts
  • Access to established business networks and industry connections
  • Brand leverage from existing TechHCad reputation and credibility
  • Administrative and back-office support systems

5. Founder Compensation: Monthly Salary Requirement

Proposed Monthly Salary: ?30,000

Strategic Rationale:

A fixed monthly salary is essential for ensuring sustained productivity, strategic focus, and professional execution. This compensation structure addresses fundamental economic realities that directly impact venture success:

5.1 Productivity & Focus Benefits

  • Elimination of financial stress: Removes survival-mode thinking that diverts cognitive resources from strategic work
  • Full-time commitment: Enables complete dedication to venture growth without need for side income sources
  • Consistent output: Financial stability supports regular work schedules and deadline adherence
  • Mental bandwidth: Frees cognitive capacity for creative problem-solving and strategic planning rather than financial concerns

5.2 Professional Execution Standards

  • Long-term planning capability: Financial security enables multi-month strategic initiatives rather than reactive short-term decisions
  • Professional presence: Maintains appropriate appearance, resources, and credibility in stakeholder meetings
  • Quality standards: Allows investment in professional development, tools, and resources that enhance work quality
  • Reliability: Ensures consistent availability and responsiveness without financial pressure to prioritize other income

5.3 Risk Management & Retention

  • Founder retention: Competitive compensation reduces risk of operational founder departure during critical growth phases
  • Opportunity cost coverage: Compensates for alternative employment opportunities foregone to build the venture
  • Motivation alignment: Combines immediate compensation (salary) with long-term incentives (equity) for balanced motivation structure

6.1 Equity Allocation Rationale

This distribution balances investment risk with operational responsibility:

Contribution Factor IRA Labs (45%) TechHCad (55%)
Financial Investment Minimal capital investment 100% of financial capital
Operational Responsibility 80% of daily execution Strategic oversight & support
Time Commitment Full-time (40-60 hours/week) Part-time oversight & guidance
Intellectual Property Course content, curriculum, systems Brand, network, infrastructure
Risk Profile Career opportunity cost Financial capital risk

6.2 Why This Structure Ensures Success

  • Balanced incentives: Both parties have substantial ownership stake in long-term success
  • Recognizes operational load: 45% equity acknowledges that I am building and executing 80% of daily operations
  • Rewards investment: 55% majority for TechHCad appropriately compensates financial risk and resource provision
  • Prevents founder burnout: Significant equity ownership motivates sustained full-time commitment through challenging growth phases
  • Facilitates decision-making: Clear majority holder (TechHCad) streamlines critical decisions while maintaining founder voice
  • Market standard alignment: This split reflects common structures in early-stage ventures with sweat equity + capital investment models

7. Strategic Benefits of Proposed Structure

7.1 For TechHCad

  • Operational excellence: Dedicated full-time founder ensuring professional execution across all functions
  • Capital efficiency: Competitive salary structure relative to market rates for equivalent executive talent
  • Reduced oversight burden: Autonomous operational management frees TechHCad for strategic focus
  • Majority control: 55% equity maintains decision-making authority and protects investment interests

7.2 For the Venture

  • Founder commitment: Meaningful equity stake ensures long-term dedication and prevents early departure
  • Quality execution: Salary removes financial distractions, enabling focus on excellence
  • Sustainable growth: Balanced compensation (salary + equity) supports both immediate operations and long-term building
  • Partnership stability: Fair structure reduces future disputes and maintains positive working relationship

8. Commitment & Vision Statement

Under this proposed structure, I commit to:

  • Full-time dedication: Complete professional focus on building and scaling the venture
  • Operational excellence: Maintaining highest standards across product development, operations, marketing, and customer engagement
  • Long-term partnership: Building the venture as a multi-year commitment, not a short-term project
  • Professional discipline: Consistent execution, transparent communication, and systematic progress tracking
  • Strategic ambition: Positioning the brand among India's leading technology education and robotics companies

Shared Vision: This partnership structure creates the foundation for building a globally competitive AI and robotics education brand that delivers exceptional value to students, institutions, and stakeholders while ensuring sustainable profitability and long-term growth for both partners.

  3. Establishment of operational bank accounts and financial systems
  4. Detailed project timeline and milestone definition for first 12 months
  5. Regular review meetings to ensure alignment and address challenges
