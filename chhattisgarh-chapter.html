About the Chhattisgarh Chapter

The IRA Labs Chhattisgarh Chapter is a regional extension of IRA Labs India.

Dedicated to empowering school & college students with robotics, electronics, AI, and STEM skills. Our mission is simple: Make world-class robotics education accessible to every student in Chhattisgarh.

This chapter is operated and managed by certified IRA Chapter Leaders who follow the official IRA curriculum, content delivery, and student support models.
